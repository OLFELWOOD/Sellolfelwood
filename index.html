    About Us

    OLFEL WOOD,“The Tree Aesthetics”is a creative collective by NONE Olivier and IRADUKUNDA Felix.
    Our wood Kitchen utensils manufacturing company is committed to providing high-quality,durable, and stylish furniture pieces that enhance the beauty of any living space. We specialize in crafting Kitchen utensils from the finest wood materials available, ensuring that each piece is not only visually appealing but also long-lasting.

    At OLFEL WOOD, we believe in sustainability and environmental responsibility. That's why we source our wood from suppliers who practice sustainable forestry, ensuring that our Kitchen utensils are made from responsibly harvested materials. We also employ eco-friendly manufacturing processes, minimizing our carbon footprint and reducing waste.
    We are committed to providing excellent customer service, and our team is always available to answer any questions and provide assistance throughout the buying process. With our Kitchen utensils, you can transform your living space into a beautiful and comfortable haven that reflects your unique style and taste.
